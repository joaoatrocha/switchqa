# Demonstration #02: Cube Volume

## Problem:

Calculate a cube volume given its side.

## Example

Input:

    side = 5

Output:

    125

## Pseudocode

### Aproach

```pseudocode
INICIO
    ED: aresta, volume INTEIRO 
    
    LER (aresta)
    volume <- (aresta ^ 3) 
    
    ESCREVER (volume)
FIM
```
